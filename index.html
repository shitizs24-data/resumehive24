Why Most Resumes Get Rejected

The common mistakes costing you interviews.

Failed ATS Screening

Over 70% of resumes are discarded by Applicant Tracking Systems before a human ever sees them due to poor formatting or lack of keywords.

Poor Readability

Recruiters spend an average of 6 seconds scanning a resume. Cluttered layouts and walls of text make them skip yours instantly.

Duty-Driven, Not Impactful

Simply listing what you did rather than the impact you made (quantifiable results) fails to show your real value to employers.

Using the same outdated, free online templates as thousands of other candidates makes blending in guaranteed.
